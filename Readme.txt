The goal:

you are harvesting fruits from a tree.  trunk and branches are denoted by 0.  fruits are denoted by value > 0.  You job is to create a robot crawler that will start from the root of the three, climb up every branch, weigh and cut every fruit. 

by the end tree is expected to contain no fruits.  your job is to keep track of how much you have collected.
